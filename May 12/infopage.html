A visit to Yellowstone National Park is an experience like none other on the planet. This was the worlds first national park for this very reason. Your day here is filled with geysers, bears, elk, lakes, rivers, mountains, and of course... TROUT!

What kind of fish are there in Yellowstone?
Brown Trout, Rainbow Trout, Greyling and Mackinaw.
Is it dangerous to fish amongst the wildlife?
Safe is a relative term. By adhereing to certain protocols of keeping distance, observing warning signs, and carrying bear spray will keep you relatively safe.
